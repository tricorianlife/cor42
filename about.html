About Cor42

Healthcare technology,
made human.

We are the IT readiness layer between healthcare technology vendors and the labs and hospitals that deploy them. From infrastructure design to LIMS rules, we get complex healthcare deployments live, on time and built to last.

Origin

Why We Created Cor42

Cor42 was born from a simple but powerful belief: healthcare technology should work seamlessly for the people who rely on it most. Patients.

We spent two decades watching brilliant technology stall on the way to the bench. The gap was rarely the science. It was the readiness, the coordination, the quiet work that makes a deployment land. So we built the partner we wished was in the room.

Our purpose

Bridging Vision and Execution in Healthcare

Patient-centred and operational in equal measure. Every project judged by one question: does this get better technology to patients faster, and keep it running once it is there?

The pattern was always the same. Ambitious technology stalling at the IT readiness gap. Vendor install teams blocked by infrastructure or change boards. Lab IT teams stretched thin. Innovation slipping by quarters.

We close that gap from both sides. For vendors, accelerated time to revenue. For labs and hospitals, a trusted advisor holding the programme together.
