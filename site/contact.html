Before you write

The questions we get asked first.

Do you resell software?

No. Vendor-agnostic by design. No referral fees, no partner commissions, no upsell. Independent advice paid for only by the client who engages us.

How do you engage?

Fixed-scope assessments, embedded delivery, programme governance, advisory retainers and LIMS rule-writing sprints. Shaped to the problem.

How fast can you start?

Discovery sprints typically begin within two to three weeks. Incident-grade engagements can start the same week.

Do you sign NDAs?

Always, before any technical detail. Mutual NDAs as standard. Vendor names, site details and architecture stay confidential.

Will you travel?

Yes for embedded engagements, accreditation events and key go-lives. Most discovery and design work is remote-first.

Do you work directly with labs as well as vendors?

Yes, this is core. We advise labs and hospitals adopting new healthcare technology, hold vendors accountable, and write LIMS rules independent of any vendor.
